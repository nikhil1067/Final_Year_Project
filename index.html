"Here at Teach for Tomorrow,

we work towards organizing a community of such individuals who can spare
some of their daily time for the betterment of lives of those who are a little less
fortunate."

"Teachers have three loves: love of learning, love of learners, and the love of bringing the first two loves together."     –Scott Hayden

It’s a platform for people who are willing and passionate about teaching students for better tomorrow.

This project aims at bridging the gap among these passionate individuals and the ones who are unable to lift the costly burden of quality education.

"The best teachers are the ones that change their minds."
                      –Terry Heick

According to a study, more than 30% of educational funds are allocated towards higher education, leaving the primary education in India in sway.

India is fourth among the top 10 nations with the highest numbers of out-of children in primary level.

Children living in slums and in small villages would be our prime beneficiary from sessions provided by people who are experienced in their skills and elementary subjects.
